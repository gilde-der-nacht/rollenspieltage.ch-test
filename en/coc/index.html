Code of Conduct

It is important to us that the Luzerner Rollenspieltage will be remembered as a beautiful and safe event for all visitors. Accordingly, we will not tolerate any behavior that is perceived as harassment or abuse, including sexism, racism, anti-semitism, homophobia or transphobia, as well as discrimination based on disability, age, appearance, background or religion.

If a person behaves in a way that makes you or someone else feel insecure, uncomfortable or unwelcome, please notify the organizers, identified by their bright, colorful shirts. Your concerns will be handled with respect and discretion.
